Image Compression Tips

  • For websites, aim for 70-80% quality to balance size and visual quality
  • WebP format typically offers 25-35% smaller files than JPG/PNG
  • Resize large images before compression for better results
  • PNG is best for images with transparency or simple graphics
  • JPG is ideal for photographs and complex images
